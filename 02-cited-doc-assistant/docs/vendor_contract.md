# Vendor Contract Summary — Cloud Storage Agreement

Contract reference: VND-2023-881
Effective: March 2023 through March 2026

## Service Level

The vendor guarantees 99.9% monthly uptime. Credits apply if uptime falls below 99.5%.

## Data Residency

Customer data is stored in US-East and EU-West regions only. Cross-region replication requires written customer approval.

## Termination

Either party may terminate with 60 days written notice. Customer data export must be completed within 30 days after termination.

## Liability Cap

Total liability is capped at twelve months of fees paid in the preceding year.

## Support Hours

Premium support is available 24/7 for severity-1 incidents. Severity-2 incidents receive response within four business hours.
