# New Hire Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the team. This guide covers your first 30 days.

## Day One Checklist

- Collect badge and laptop from IT in Building A, Room 210.
- Complete HR paperwork in the employee portal.
- Meet your onboarding buddy for a campus tour.
- Set up email, Slack, and VPN credentials with IT support.

## Benefits Enrollment

Benefits enrollment must be completed within 30 days of your start date. Medical, dental, and vision plans begin on the first day of the month following enrollment.

## Training Requirements

All hires must complete security awareness training within the first week. Role-specific training modules are assigned by your manager and must be finished within 30 days.

## Probation Period

New employees serve a 90-day probation period. Performance check-ins occur at 30, 60, and 90 days.
