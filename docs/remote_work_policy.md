# Employee Remote Work Policy

Effective date: January 2024

## Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed their probation period are eligible for remote work up to three days per week. Managers must approve the weekly schedule in writing.

## Equipment

The company provides a laptop, monitor, and VPN access. Employees must use company-approved devices for all work-related tasks. Personal devices may not store confidential client data.

## Core Hours

Remote employees must be available during core hours from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in their local time zone. Meetings outside core hours require 24-hour notice.

## Security Requirements

When working remotely, employees must connect through the corporate VPN, enable full-disk encryption, and lock their screen when away from the device. Violations are handled under the Information Security SOP.

## Expense Reimbursement

Internet stipends of $50 per month are available for employees who work remotely at least two days per week. Submit receipts through the expense portal by the 5th of each month.
